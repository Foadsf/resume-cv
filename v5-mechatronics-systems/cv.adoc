== Professional Experience

=== System Lead Engineer, NTS D&E, Hengelo
_Mar 2022-Present_

* Owned qualification tooling workstreams for ASML modules, connecting pressure decay, particle validation, electrical and mechanical checks, HMI behavior, and acceptance criteria.
* Performed system engineering for the EXE:5200 sensor assembly, including design kickoff, part-numbering, and release-planning coordination.
* Bridged mockup design, hardware-in-the-loop testing, and software validation needs in the Safety PLC Emulator workstream.
* Resolved a fatigue life risk in Inconel 718 E-pin folded leaf springs by tracing failures to EDM recast layer, coordinating plasma polishing and Kolsterising/K33 trials, and validating 2x life improvement on representative leaf springs; recognized at ASML's Technology Conference 2024.

=== Lead Mechanical Engineer, VDL ETG T&D

* Led EXE5000 cable slab integration tooling workstreams across fixtures, hose and cable preforming, molds, CAD interfaces, supplier feedback, and manufacturability closure.
* Interpreted bellows, pressure-drop, stiffness, and force-decoupler behavior for early-stage ATCM/VES mechanical concepts.
