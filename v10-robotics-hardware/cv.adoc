== Professional Experience

=== System Lead Engineer, NTS D&E, Hengelo
_Mar 2022-Present_

* Led the mockup design and hardware-in-the-loop testing for the Safety PLC Emulator, validating safety-critical PLC logic without production hardware.
* Owned qualification tooling workstreams for ASML modules, connecting pressure decay, particle validation, electrical and mechanical checks, HMI behavior, and acceptance criteria.
* Performed system engineering for the EXE:5200 SCFS sensor assembly, including design kickoff, part-numbering, and release-planning coordination.
* Resolved a fatigue-life risk in Inconel 718 E-pin folded leaf springs by tracing failures to EDM recast layer, coordinating plasma polishing and Kolsterising/K33 trials, and validating 2x life improvement on representative leaf springs; recognized at ASML's Technology Conference 2024.

=== Lead Mechanical Engineer, VDL ETG T&D
_Jul 2020-Feb 2022_

* Led EXE5000 cable-slab integration tooling across fixtures, hose and cable preforming, molds, CAD interfaces, supplier feedback, and manufacturability closure.
* Developed precision-mechanics concepts and calculation packages for YieldStar 500 fiducial interfaces, including leaf-spring stiffness, geometry trade-offs, and tolerance-sensitive optical/mechatronic interfaces.
* Interpreted bellows, pressure-drop, stiffness, and force-decoupler behavior for early-stage ATCM/VES mechanical concepts.

=== PhD Researcher, University of Twente / TU Eindhoven
_2016-2020_

* Designed and built MRI-compatible pneumatic stepper actuators, patient-mounted robot concepts, sensor interfaces, and experimental setups, validated on custom test benches.
* Contributed to the EU Horizon 2020 MURAB consortium on MRI and ultrasound robotic assisted biopsy, with a EUR 4.34M project budget.
* Authored peer-reviewed work on MRI-compatible pneumatic actuation and holds patents on MRI-compatible surgery assistant and drug-injection robot concepts.
