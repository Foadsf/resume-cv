== Profile

I'm a systems engineer and a lifelong inventor. I filed my first two robotics patents in Iran in my twenties, then designed a robot motor that runs on nothing but air, no metal, no electronics, fast enough to drive a surgical robot inside a live MRI scanner. I bring that blank-page-to-shipped instinct to the world's most demanding precision manufacturing, ASML's lithography machines, where I solve the hard physical problems, build the engineering capabilities, and open the funding that lets a company punch above its weight. I move fluently across the whole stack: systems architecture, CAD/CAE/CAM/PLM, mechanism and actuation design, manufacturing, and the teams that make it real.

== Signature Achievements

* *Invented PneuAct*, a new class of MRI-compatible pneumatic stepper motor. No metal, no electronics, no lubrication, fully 3D-printed, 850 RPM. IEEE ICRA 2018 and RA-L 2020; featured on Hackaday and U-Today.
* *Doubled the fatigue life* of a critical ASML flexure by root-causing a failure everyone else had only patched (EDM recast layer) and fixing it at the metallurgy. ASML credited the result at its 2024 Technology Conference.
* *Built the Research Funding Initiative* at NTS from a blank page, a pipeline that connects the company's engineering to EU and national R&D money and put ~40 funding opportunities on the table.
