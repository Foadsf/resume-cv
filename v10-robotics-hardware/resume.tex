\section{Professional Summary}
Hands-on engineer across the full robotics hardware stack, mechanism and actuator design, sensing, control-oriented experiments, and the precision-manufacturing and test tooling that turns prototypes into validated, buildable hardware. PhD in MRI-compatible surgical robotics: designed, built, and experimentally validated novel pneumatic stepper actuators and patient-mounted robots (two patents, IEEE ICRA and RA-L publications). Since then industrialized robot-grade precision hardware at ASML Tier-1 suppliers (VDL ETG, NTS), owning integration tooling, qualification tooling, and hardware-in-the-loop validation.
